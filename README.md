## Key Findings
- PYUSD shows steady daily transfer volume with periodic high-value institutional movements.
- Weekly active wallets indicate growing adoption.
- PYUSD maintains a tight peg near $1.00.
- Median on-chain transaction fees are low, though spikes occur during congestion.
- Transaction success rates are consistently near 100%.

---

## Recommendation
PYUSD is suitable for pilot treasury payments where speed and transparency matter,
with ongoing monitoring of Ethereum network congestion and liquidity concentration.
